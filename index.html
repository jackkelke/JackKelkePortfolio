Significant Projects

GTI WideBody Kit

Click on a project image to flip and see details!

A deep passion of mine is old german cars, the Porsches, the BMWs, and the Volkswagens, I love the boxy styling of these 80's cars and I really enjoy imagining what they could look like with my own spin on it. Here is my 1983 Volkswagen GTI I purchased to overhaul with my own style, particularly a wide body kit and some new color. I have been wanting to make a body kit for a vehicle for a very long time and I have finally done it for this car. Transportation / Automotive design is what I truley love and want to do as a career.

Here is the first step I took in designing my custom wide body kit, 3D scanning. 3D scanning allowed me to get perfect dimensional accuracy while designing my kit. This was critical when it came to 3D modeling. This took place in my garage and took many attempts but I was eventually able to get a perfect mesh that I could inport to my modeling software. 3D scanning was pretty difficult but I deeply believe this is a very valuable skill to posses in the field of automotive design.

After 3D scanning, the next step I took was 3D modeling! This stage was my favorite, being able to create any concept I like is truely so satisfying. While the design took quite a few days to complete it was well worth it. This step helped teach me alot about using the application Fusion360, a very valuable tool for any designer. I used the forms application within fusion to help make the dynamic shapes and fit them to the car for a percise fit.

The next staged called for 3D printing. Being able to bring my 3d models to life with 3D printing is so satisfying, and watching the bodykit slowly be produced in essentially puzzel pieces was amazing. The ability to prototype and test versions was great. While there were many failed parts and set backs, I learned alot about 3D printing during this process and how to optimize printing and the results.

The Final Product! This took countless hours and so much post processing but the final result is something I would not of believed I was capable of. I completely brought this project from my mind to reality in a few months. So many skills were learned during this project and I cannot wait to do it again and even better than before. Not only did I learn a bunch of new skills like 3D scanning, printing, and modeling, I learned about how im going to do it better next time.
